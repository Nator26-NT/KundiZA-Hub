Room Booking Guidelines

+ Requirements

1. Booking Requirements
  • Reservations must be made at least 5 days in advance.
  • Please specify your preferred room setup:
    • Classroom
    • Banquet Round
    • Theatre
    • Other (please specify): __________
    • Please include any additional needs, such as catering, expected attendance, Microsoft TEAMS, and infrastructure, in your request.

    2. Confirmation
    • Your booking is only confirmed upon receiving communication from Kerron Pillay via email or phone.

    3. Duration Limits
    • Bookings are limited to a maximum of five consecutive days.

    4. Attendee List
    • Submit your attendee list at least three days before the event to ensure security protocols are in place.

    5. Technology Access
    • Meeting rooms are equipped with Microsoft TEAMS and HDMI connections.

    6. Cancellations/Rescheduling
    • Please notify us of any cancellations or rescheduling at your earliest convenience to allow other bookings.
+ Additional Requirements

Photography & Video Recording
  • Photography or videography is allowed with prior approval from IBM’s Communications Department. A Camera Permit form must be completed on-site following approval.

Event Display Options
  • A Welcome/Overview slide may be displayed in alignment with IBM guidelines, provided it is shared 24 hours in advance.
  • Marketing materials, such as banners or flyers, are allowed in designated areas.

Guest Wi-Fi Access
  • IBM offers guest access to our “Guest Wi-Fi.”
+ Catering

Catering Arrangements
  • Event organizers are responsible for arranging catering through IBM-recommended local suppliers, who have been vetted and approved.
  • Alcohol is not permitted on IBM premises.
  • All catering areas must be left clean; if additional cleaning is needed, a fee will apply.
+ Post-Event Responsibilities

Post-Event Responsibilities

Clean-Up
  • Event organizers are responsible for ensuring that the room is left clean and orderly after the event. Any additional cleaning required will incur a fee.
+ Equipment Handling

Equipment Handling
  • If any IBM-provided equipment (e.g., projectors, AV systems) is used, please handle it with care. Report any damages or technical issues to the IBM KundiZA Hub team immediately.

Personal Belongings
  • IBM is not responsible for any lost or stolen personal belongings. Please ensure all personal items are collected before leaving.

Accessibility
  • IBM is dedicated to accommodating guests with disabilities. Please inform us of any accessibility requirements ahead of your event.
+ Emergency Procedure

Emergency Procedures

Fire Exits
  • Fire exits are clearly marked throughout the building. Familiarize yourself with the nearest exit routes and assembly points.

Evacuation

  • In case of an emergency, follow IBM staff instructions and proceed calmly to the designated assembly area.
